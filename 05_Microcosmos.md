## 5.1 Mechanism of Consciousness: Edge of Chaos
Regarding the origin of consciousness, this protocol takes a cautious stance on Penrose's "Quantum Brain Theory" and instead supports Chaos Theory as a macroscopic dynamic process.

* **Chaotic Itinerancy:**

* **Qualia and Dynamic Equilibrium:**
    As Ken Mogi suggests, consciousness is not a static list of information but the "process of dynamic equilibrium itself" woven by metabolism and information processing in the brain.
